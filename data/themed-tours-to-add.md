# 5 themed sightseeing tours to add via /admin/activities

These match the competitor's themed line-up using stops Belle Mare Tours already covers.
Create each in **Admin → Tours → New** with **type = activity**, **category = Sightseeing tours**,
**pricing mode = vehicle** (so it uses the global sightseeing vehicle prices: Sedan €70 / SUV €85 /
Family €85 / Van €125 / Coaster €225). Duration ≈ full day (8 h). Languages: English, French.
Pickup available: yes. Add 4–6 photos per tour.

Standard inclusions for all: private air-conditioned vehicle, English/French driver-guide, hotel
pickup & drop-off island-wide, fuel & parking. Exclusions: entrance fees, lunch, personal expenses.

## 1. Tea & Wildlife Tour
**Summary:** A full day through the green central highlands — tea country, a colonial estate and Mauritian wildlife.
**Highlights / stops:** Bois Chéri tea plantation & factory (tea tasting) · Saint Aubin colonial estate · La Vanille Nature Park (giant tortoises, crocodiles) · Gris Gris cliffs (Souillac) · Rochester Falls.
**Description:** Discover the cooler, greener heart of the island. Walk the tea fields and taste at Bois Chéri, tour a 19th-century sugar estate, meet giant tortoises and crocodiles at La Vanille, and finish at the dramatic Gris Gris cliffs and Rochester Falls.

## 2. Mauritian History & Heritage Tour
**Summary:** The island's history — indenture, sugar, and colonial Port Louis — in a day.
**Highlights / stops:** Aapravasi Ghat (UNESCO) · L'Aventure du Sucre sugar museum · Port Louis (Place d'Armes, Government House) · Marie Reine de la Paix · Fort Adelaide (Citadel) · Cap Malheureux red church.
**Description:** Trace Mauritius from indentured-labour history at the UNESCO Aapravasi Ghat and the L'Aventure du Sucre museum, through colonial Port Louis and the Citadel's harbour views, to the iconic red-roofed church at Cap Malheureux.

## 3. Port Louis City Tour
**Summary:** A half/full-day exploration of the capital — markets, waterfront and viewpoints.
**Highlights / stops:** Central Market (Bazaar) · Caudan Waterfront · Chinatown · Aapravasi Ghat · Champ de Mars racecourse · Fort Adelaide (Citadel) · Blue Penny Museum.
**Description:** Dive into Mauritius's lively capital: haggle in the Central Market, stroll the Caudan Waterfront, wander Chinatown, and take in Port Louis from the Citadel before visiting the Blue Penny Museum.

## 4. North Beach-Hopping Tour
**Summary:** The best of the north coast's beaches and seaside villages in one relaxed day.
**Highlights / stops:** Mont Choisy · Trou aux Biches · Pereybère · Grand Baie (La Cuvette) · Cap Malheureux (red church) · Bain Boeuf.
**Description:** A laid-back day along the north's finest sands — swim and snorkel at Trou aux Biches and Mont Choisy, relax at Pereybère and La Cuvette, and photograph the red-roofed chapel at Cap Malheureux looking out to Coin de Mire.

## 5. South-West Splendour Tour
**Summary:** Mountains, the seven-coloured earth and clifftop viewpoints across the wild south-west.
**Highlights / stops:** Grand Bassin (Ganga Talao sacred lake) · Black River Gorges viewpoint · Chamarel Seven Coloured Earth & waterfall · Le Morne viewpoint · Maconde viewpoint · Tamarin salt pans.
**Description:** The scenic south-west in a day: the sacred lake at Grand Bassin, sweeping views over the Black River Gorges, the geological wonder of Chamarel's seven-coloured earths and waterfall, and the UNESCO-listed Le Morne from the Maconde clifftop.
